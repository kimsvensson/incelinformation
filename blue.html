>INCEL DICTIONARY

DEFINITION

incel
/ˈɪnsɛl/
noun

* Short for «involuntary celibate»
A member of an online community of young men who consider themselves unable to attract women sexually, despite their desire to do so.

* The term originated in online forums in the 90s and has since been adopted by certain communities as a self-identifier.

VOCABULARY

Chad: This term is used to describe men who are seen as highly attractive and successful with women. Incels often view Chads as their main competition for sexual partners and may express resentment or envy toward them.

Stacy: This term is used to describe women who are seen as highly attractive and sexually promiscuous. Incels often view Stacys as shallow and superficial, and may blame them for their inability to find sexual partners.

Beta: This term is used to describe men who are seen as unattractive or unsuccessful with women. Incels often view themselves as Betas and may express frustration or anger at their perceived lack of sexual opportunities.

Femoid: This derogatory term is used to describe women and is meant to dehumanize and objectify them. It is often used in a highly derogatory or hateful manner.

Looksmaxing: This term refers to the practice of improving one's physical appearance in order to become more attractive to potential partners. Incels often place a high emphasis on looksmaxing, believing that physical attractiveness is the most important factor in attracting sexual partners.
