* Incels have a strong online presence, with many congregating in forums and chat groups to share stories and vent frustrations, and reinforce their beliefs.

* These communities, which include forums, message boards, and social media groups, may also serve as recruitment grounds for extremist groups that promote misogyny and violence.

OSTRACIZED

* Despite their online presence, incels are often the target of ridicule and mockery by other online communities, such as those who promote positive body image and self-esteem.

* As a result of their harmful behavior and attitudes, incels have been banned from a number of online forums and platforms, including Reddit. In 2017, Reddit banned the r/incels subreddit for violating the platform's content policy on promoting violence and harassment.
